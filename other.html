Community Curator

In this cozy social sim, you play as a museum curator trying to bring everyone in the community together through working to rebuild the museum collections.

A mobile game for helping with ADHD and ASD traits as well as overall quality of life improvement for those impacted by the characteristics.

Done as thesis project, from research-based game designing to implementation, both coding and creation of all graphics, and testing with neurodiverse players.

A Viagem de D. Beatriz

Explore part of the ship taking Beatrice of Portugal, Duchess of Savoy, on her trip in 1521 and find real historical artifacts that could have been found on it.

Done as part of a Master's class, focused on teamwork and management while following a client's requirements, in this case a list of items found in old documents that the player could learn about by finding them in the places they would be in.

Tudo Ao Mesmo Tempo

Full design done for a Master's class, which aims to simulate the experience of different traits and behaviors associated with Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder, through various minigames. These include: Bad Memory, Mental and Physical Hyperactivity, Inattention, Impulsivity, Emotional Disregulation and Sensory Sensitivity.

In-development prototype that will be available in Portuguese and English.

Foraging Riddles

You, as the player, have to help your grandma forage different types of mushroom, while being careful with the wolves that roam the area around the cottage, for they trick passersby into answering their riddles.

Done as a Master's class exercise, this very early prototype/ proof-of-concept aims to test out a very basic version of the mechanics and concept of survival, foraging mushrooms and fighting through riddles, and it requires many improvements to the game's balancing, graphics, and overall gameplay.

Game design exercise done for a Master's class with the a complete Game Design Document being developed, including the full story and all puzzles. The prototype implemented only the first challenge level, with further iterations needed to improve feedback to the player and the overall experience.
